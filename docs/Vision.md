# Rover Explorer Vision

## Elevator Pitch

Rover Explorer is a 2.5D side-scrolling planetary exploration game where players pilot increasingly advanced rovers across hostile alien worlds in search of resources, artifacts, scientific discoveries, and hidden secrets.

## Core Pillars

### Exploration

Players should constantly feel compelled to see what lies beyond the next ridge, cave, or horizon.

### Discovery

Scanning, uncovering artifacts, and documenting alien worlds should be rewarding.

### Survival

Environmental hazards should create tension and meaningful decision-making.

### Progression

Every expedition should contribute toward unlocking new capabilities and new worlds.

Easy to learn.

Difficult to master.

Exploration first.

Combat second.

Every expedition should reveal something new.
